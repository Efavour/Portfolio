New Book Release: Inspiration and Process in Design

Our latest book, Louise Fili: Inspiration and Process in Design, is part of a Moleskine/Princeton Architectural Press series which also showcases Paul Rand, Seymour Chwast, and Milton Glaser. Profusely illustrated with sketches, reference, and comps for personal and commercial projects, it even includes practical advice for how to make a proper script out of cooked bucatini.

Best Selling Shadow Type

Shadow Type: Classic Three-Dimensional Lettering presents hundreds of specimens—advertisements, shop signs, billboards, movie titles, type-specimen sheets—drawn from the nineteenth to the mid-twentieth century, a particularly rich period in the history of dramatic shaded type.
